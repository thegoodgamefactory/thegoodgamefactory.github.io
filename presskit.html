Elevator Pitch

You've escaped the lab but at what cost? As you're leaving, you have a sudden change of heart. You must collect the evidence and expose this place for what it is, bringing an end to what goes on here.

Mr figs is a topdown 2D action puzzler set in an ethereal realm of dungeons, ice-caverns, lava and more.

  • - Bomb your way to the truth
  • - Explore 100+ levels across 4 areas
  • - Find every piece of evidence and expose your creator
  • - Discover hidden rooms, challenge rooms and bosses!
